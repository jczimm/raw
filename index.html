RAW Tools Suite

A collection of tools inspired by the idea of isolating listening from visual biases.

raudio

A SoundCloud client that isolates track posts to their audio.

just music—no cover art, no waveform—and a bucket, which you can fill with the music you collect

Blind

A Max for Live plugin that covers an Ableton Live set while it's playing to filter out clip and arrangement visuals from the production and previewing experience.

RAW skin

A playful, minimalist skin for Ableton Live 8/9, based on raudio's color scheme.

includes light and dark versions
